R&D | Production | Investment

Founded in 2015 in Ukraine, Black Mark Corp. stands as an innovative R&D center within the armaments sector, having developed over 40 successful products. These items are manufactured by our partners in various countries, with some exclusively produced in-house. Over the span of 8 years, we have amassed significant expertise in engineering, ballistics, and explosives handling.

Our professional focus revolves around addressing specific narrowly-targeted tactical challenges, which encompass the production of cartridges including less lethal slugs, sniper rounds, UAV ordnance, UAVs, specialized electronics, and more.
